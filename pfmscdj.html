MSc Computational and Data Journalism

Selection Rules:

Students should select one Computer Science (CMT***) module, and two Journalism (MCT***) modules in the spring semester. Of the Journalism modules, students should select either:

  • Two modules from MCT481, MCT494, MCT528 and MCT498

or:

  • One module from MCT481, MCT494, MCT528 and MCT498, and one module from MCT556, MCT554, MCT555, MCT557 and MCT558
